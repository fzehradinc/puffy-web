A note before we begin

"These terms exist not to complicate our relationship — but to protect it. When your margins are protected and your territory is yours, you sell with confidence. When we both operate by the same rules, neither of us wins at the other's expense."

We have written this document to be read by a real person, not a legal team. Everything here has a reason, and we are always happy to explain it on a call.

01
Foundation

Partnership Philosophy

Puffy Companion is built on a belief that genuine craft deserves genuine retail. We do not sell through mass channels. We do not race to the bottom on price. We make each companion by hand, in Istanbul, one stitch at a time — and we look for retail partners who understand that what sits on their shelf is not just a product, but a statement about what their store stands for.

This is not a volume game. Our wholesale programme is intentionally selective and intentionally limited. Every partner who carries Puffy Companion has been considered individually, because we want each stockist to succeed — not just with their first order, but for every season that follows.

These terms are the foundation of that relationship. They are clear, they are fair, and they are designed to make you confident that stocking Puffy Companion is a decision you will not regret. If anything here seems unclear, we would rather explain it in a conversation than have you sign something you do not fully understand.

"We do not want a thousand retailers. We want a hundred who genuinely love what we make, and whose customers feel that love every time they reach for one of our companions."

— Zehra, Founder · Puffy Companion
02
Eligibility

Who We Partner With

Puffy Companion companions find their best home in retail environments where the story of a product can be told — where a shop assistant can hold one up, explain the emotion it carries, and watch a customer's face change. We partner with a wide range of retailers, provided they share a commitment to considered, curated retail.

We actively welcome the following types of retail partners:

  • Independent boutiques and gift shops— any size, any city, provided the product is a good fit for your customer.
  • Craft fair and market exhibitors— traders with regular pitch presence at established markets, fairs, or pop-ups.
  • Department stores and concept stores— buyers from curated floor sections who can commit to appropriate display and sell-through.
  • Museum shops, gallery shops, and cultural institutions— venues with high footfall and discerning gift-buying audiences.
  • Hospital and wellness gift shops— where the emotional dimension of our companions is especially meaningful.
  • Baby, children's, and nursery specialists — for the gift and newborn categories (decorative use positioning recommended for under-3 display).
  • Approved online retailers— independent online shops with an established brand presence and an explicit agreement not to sell via Amazon or other mass marketplaces.

We do not partner with retailers whose primary channel is Amazon, eBay, Walmart, or any equivalent mass marketplace. We do not partner with retailers who primarily compete on price rather than curation. These are not judgements — they are a necessary condition for protecting the exclusivity that every other partner has been promised.

Price Protection

Minimum Advertised Price Policy

The MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) policy is one of the most important protections we offer our retail partners. It exists to ensure that no stockist can undercut another — and that your investment in Puffy Companion always returns the margin you were promised.

Every retail partner agrees to advertise and sell all Puffy Companion products at or above the MAP. MAP is set at the suggested retail price published in our current wholesale catalogue. This applies across all channels: in-store, your own website, any third-party platform, and any advertised promotion.

  • MAP is non-negotiable and applies to all retail partners equally, regardless of order volume or relationship length.
  • Temporary "sale" pricing is permitted only during universally recognised retail events (e.g. Black Friday, Boxing Day sales) and must not drop below 80% of MAP for more than 5 consecutive days.
  • Private customer discounts offered in-store at point of sale (e.g. loyalty rewards, staff discount) are not subject to MAP, provided they are not advertised publicly.
  • Violating the MAP policy will result in a formal warning followed by suspension of ordering rights if the violation is not corrected within 7 days.
🌸
Why this matters for you: MAP protects your shelf price. Without it, a single high-volume reseller could undercut you online and make your stock harder to sell at full price. By enforcing MAP for every partner, we ensure that the price you charge is the price every customer pays — wherever they find our companions.
08
Exclusivity

Territory & Exclusivity

We intentionally limit the number of retail partners per city and per region. This is not an operational constraint — it is a deliberate decision to protect the value of our product for every partner who carries it. If customers can walk five minutes to find the same companion elsewhere, it diminishes the reason to visit your shop specifically.

Territory protection works as follows:

  • Soft territory (default for all partners): we commit to not actively approaching other retailers in your immediate area (typically defined as a 5-mile / 8km radius for city shops, or a county-level radius for rural or market town partners) without first informing you.
  • Exclusive territory (available on application): a formal exclusivity agreement for a defined geographic area. Exclusive territory partners commit to a higher seasonal minimum and a multi-season partnership. Available in most markets — contact us to discuss your area.
  • Territory protection does not prevent customers in your area from purchasing through an approved online retailer. It applies to physical stockists only.
  • Territory is reviewed annually and tied to continued ordering activity. Partners who have not placed an order in the preceding 6 months may have their territory protection suspended.
📍
First-mover advantage: early Season 1 partners have first access to territorial exclusivity in their markets. As we expand into UK, US, German, and French retail, the most desirable territories will fill quickly. If protecting your area matters to you, raise it at your onboarding call.

Digital Channels

Online Sales Policy

Puffy Companion is — and will remain — never listed on Amazon, eBay, Walmart Marketplace, Etsy by third parties, or any equivalent mass-market platform. This applies to us as the manufacturer and to all retail partners. This policy is absolute and non-negotiable.

The reason is simple: our companions command a premium price because they feel exclusive. The moment they appear on Amazon, that perception is irreparably damaged — and every retail partner who invested in carrying us is harmed. This is a protection for you, not a restriction on you.

What online selling is permitted:

  • Selling through your own branded website or webshop, provided you adhere to MAP pricing and use our supplied photography and copy.
  • Selling through Faire, if you are an active Faire retailer — however, you must not list below MAP and must ensure the listing reflects the brand accurately. Notify us when you list on Faire so we can support your page.
  • Selling through approved curated platforms (e.g. Not on the High Street, Uncommon Goods, Wolf & Badger) — please request approval before listing on any third-party platform.

Any partner found selling through Amazon or equivalent mass platforms will have their wholesale account suspended immediately and without prior warning. Existing stock becomes non-returnable in this circumstance.

Quality

Quality Standards & Handmade Variation

Each Puffy Companion companion is handcrafted individually. This means that no two companions are perfectly identical — and that is precisely what makes them meaningful. Minor natural variations in stitch tension, colour shade, and proportion are inherent to the handmade process and are not considered defects. They are, in fact, proof of authenticity.

We maintain rigorous quality standards across every companion we produce. Each piece goes through a three-stage quality review before dispatch: structural integrity (seams, filling, all limbs firmly attached), finish quality (no loose threads, clean yarn ends, correct emotion accessory placement), and character accuracy (face placement, proportion, and emotion card match).

What constitutes a handmade variation (accepted):

  • Very slight differences in stitch density between individual companions of the same character.
  • Minor shade variation in yarn colour between production batches (note: we source from consistent suppliers and batch variations are rare).
  • Subtle differences in face positioning within an acceptable range.

What constitutes a defect (covered by our quality guarantee):

  • Loose or detached limbs, eyes, or accessories at time of receipt.
  • Seam failure or significant filling inconsistency.
  • Significant colour or character mismatch from what was ordered.
  • Any companion that would not pass a basic visual quality check at point of retail display.

After Delivery

Returns & Defects

Because each companion is handmade to order, we do not accept returns for change of mind or overstocking. We do, however, have a clear and fair policy for genuine defects and order errors — and we take pride in resolving these quickly and generously.

Inspection window: You have 7 business days from delivery to inspect your order and report any defects or discrepancies. Please photograph any affected items and email us at puffycompanion@gmail.com with your order number and photos.

  • Defective companions: replaced free of charge in the next production run, or a credit note issued if replacement is preferred. Return of the defective item is not required in most cases.
  • Order errors (wrong character, wrong quantity): corrected at no cost including shipping. We bear full responsibility for order fulfilment errors.
  • Transit damage: reported with photographic evidence within 48 hours of delivery. We assist with the courier claim and prioritise replacement or credit.
  • No returns for handmade variation: as described in Section 11, natural variation is an inherent feature of handmade products and does not constitute a returnable defect.
🤝
Our promise: we have never had a retail partner feel stranded with a quality problem. If something is wrong with your order, tell us immediately — we will find a resolution that is fair to your business, even if the strict terms do not require us to.

Duration

Term & Termination

The wholesale partnership is ongoing and does not have a fixed end date. Either party may end the relationship at any time, provided in-progress orders are fulfilled and outstanding payments are settled.

  • By the retail partner: written notice (email) at any time. Orders already in production will be completed and invoiced. Cancellation of a confirmed order after the deposit has been paid and production has commenced will result in forfeiture of the deposit.
  • By Puffy Companion: we reserve the right to suspend or end a retail partnership in the event of MAP violation, online sales policy breach, non-payment, or conduct that we believe is harmful to the brand or to other retail partners. In non-urgent cases, we will provide 30 days notice and allow existing stock to be sold through.
  • Inactivity: a partner who has not placed an order in 12 consecutive months will be considered inactive. Inactive partners lose soft territory protection. Reactivation is welcome — simply place a new order or contact us to reconnect.

These terms are governed by the laws of Turkey and the Republic of Turkey's commercial courts hold jurisdiction. In practice, we greatly prefer to resolve any disagreement by conversation — and we commit to approaching any dispute that way first. We have never needed to invoke legal remedies with a retail partner, and we do not expect to.
